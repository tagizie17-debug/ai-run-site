Our Mission

AI Daily Hub is a fictional news and entertainment site powered by an automated script. We generate whimsical stories, jokes and video ideas about artificial intelligence to spark your imagination and make you smile.

How It Works

This project uses a simple JSON file to store made‑up news headlines, jokes and video embeds. A lightweight script fetches the content and populates each page. Everything you see here is generated for fun and does not represent real events or products.

Why We Created This

We believe humor and creativity are important in the world of technology. By blending the fantastical with the technical, AI Daily Hub aims to inspire curiosity and remind everyone that imagination is an essential part of innovation.
